## What you’ll find here
- **Core Projects** – full systems and applications built for real-world use.
- **Starter Kits & Templates** – clean foundations to build on quickly.
- **Utilities & Tools** – small but powerful components we actually use.
- **Guides & Systems** – practical implementations, not just theory.

### Focus areas
- **Discord Systems** – advanced bots, automation, analytics, moderation, music systems.
- **Web & Dashboards** – modern frontends + backend APIs (Node.js, PHP, React).
- **AI & Backend Systems** – scalable APIs, AI integrations, automation pipelines.
- **Games & Mods** – Roblox systems, Minecraft mods, and interactive experiences.
- **Infrastructure** – Docker, Cloudflare, server hosting, deployment pipelines.
